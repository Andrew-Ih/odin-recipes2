French Bread Pizza Recipe

Description

Savor the fusion of Italian and French flavors with our French bread pizza. Crispy French bread topped with zesty marinara, gooey cheese, and your favorite toppings. Baked to perfection, it's a quick and delicious twist on classic pizza, perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 Loaf French bread
  • 4 tbsp. butter, melted
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of marinara or pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cup of shredded mozzarella
  • Pepperoni slices
  • 2 tbsp. freshly grated Parmesan
  • Crushed red pepper flakes
  • Freshly chopped basil, for topping

Steps

    Step 1

  1. Preheat oven to 400Â°. Slice bread in half lengthwise. Mix melted butter and garlic together and brush all over cut side of bread. Bake for 10 minutes to lightly toast.

    2. Step 2

  2. Spread marinara on top and top with mozzarella, pepperoni slices, parmesan, and red pepper flakes. Bake again until cheese is melty, 10 minutes more.

    3. Step 3

  3. Top with basil to serve.
