Mac and Cheese with Bread Crumbs Recipe

Description

Indulge in our creamy mac and cheese with a crispy breadcrumb topping: a comforting classic elevated to perfection.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces elbow macaroni
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs (preferably Panko breadcrumbs)

Steps

    Step 1

  1. Cook macaroni until al dente, then drain.

    2. Step 2

  2. Make a cheese sauce with butter, flour, milk, and shredded cheddar and mozzarella cheeses.

    3. Step 3

  3. Combine macaroni and cheese sauce in a baking dish.

    4. Step 4

  4. Top with breadcrumbs.

    5. Step 5

  5. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 20-25 minutes until golden and bubbly.

    6. Step 6

  6. Serve and enjoy!
